Progress Tracking

See real PPL progress at a glance

Know exactly where you stand on every FAR 61.109 requirement. SoloTrack breaks down Total Time, Solo, Dual, Cross-Country, Night, and Instrument hours with visual progress bars and a training checklist.

  • All 6 FAR 61.109 requirement categories
  • Training checklist per FAR 61.109(a)
  • Overall progress percentage & hours-to-go
Flight Logging

Log every flight in under a minute

Smart defaults, templates for Pattern Work, Local Lesson, and Cross-Country flights, plus Quick Entry mode. Just pick a template, confirm the details, and save.

  • One-tap templates for common flight types
  • Hobbs & Tach time, landings, categories
  • Airport identifiers with route mapping
Live Tracking

Time flights as you fly

Start a session before takeoff and SoloTrack records everything in real time. The Live Activity and home screen widget keep your session visible at a glance, then one tap prefills your logbook entry.

  • iOS Live Activity on your lock screen
  • Home screen widget with live stats
  • One-tap prefill from session to logbook
Checkride Prep

Stay checkride-ready

Capture CFI signatures digitally, log advanced training details like instrument approaches, holding procedures, and course tracking. Endorsed flights are locked and tamper-proof.

  • Digital CFI signature capture
  • Advanced training fields (approaches, holds, XC distance)
  • Endorsed & locked flight records
Data Portability

Import, export, and sync

Bring in past flights from CSV, export to PDF for your CFI, and optionally sync across devices with iCloud. Duplicate detection prevents double entries, and your data stays local-first.

  • CSV import with duplicate detection
  • CSV & PDF export for printable logbooks
  • Optional end-to-end encrypted iCloud sync
